# Exemplars — worked rewrites and voice calibration

Three levels on each line: the **original draft**, the **de-slopped** version (clean but flat), and the **MBB-snappy** version (clean *and* lands). The third column is the target.

> All targets, sectors and figures below are **illustrative and invented** to teach the move. They are not real findings. In a live deliverable every figure traces to a source (Integrity rule, SKILL.md).

## Worked rewrites

| Original draft | De-slopped (clean, flat) | MBB-snappy (target) |
|---|---|---|
| "My base-case view is that AI is a net positive for this business." | "Our base case is that AI helps this business." | **"AI is the upside here, not the risk. We'd underwrite a buy."** *(earned contrast + two-beat)* |
| "The proprietary asset is the accredited content set, not the database — built over decades of standards-body endorsement that a model cannot reproduce." | "The proprietary asset is the accredited content set, not the database. Decades of endorsement is what a model cannot reproduce." | **"The moat is the accreditation, not the code. A model can write the output; it can't grant itself thirty years of standing."** |
| "The product sits upstream of a mandatory audit step; replacing it invalidates the customer's insurance cover and triggers personal liability for the responsible officer." | "The product sits upstream of the audit. Drop it and you invalidate insurance cover and put the responsible officer personally on the hook." | **"Rip it out and you void the insurance and put a named person in legal jeopardy. Nobody does that to save a licence fee."** |
| "Authoring the output requires synthesis of primary and secondary regulation across more than a dozen statutes that change on a quarterly basis." | "Authoring the output means synthesising a dozen-plus statutes that change every quarter." | **"Twelve statutes, changing quarterly, reconciled by hand. Exactly where models break and incumbents win."** |
| "The technology team grows from 22 to 29 FTE by the plan year, yet the organisation chart shows no roles explicitly labelled ML or data engineering." | "The tech team grows to 29 by the plan year, yet the org chart names no ML engineer." | **"The shipped product proves the ML capability exists. The org chart just doesn't show it. Find it in the management session."** |
| "The plan reflects conservative SaaS economics with embedded migration uplift and a meaningful band of unmodelled upside." | "The plan uses conservative SaaS economics, embedded migration uplift, and a band of unmodelled upside." | **"Conservative economics, billed a year ahead. The plan quietly hides a double-digit-million band of upside."** |
| "This represents the only credible AI-enabled disruption path: an adjacent platform vendor uses models to internally generate a competing content library." | "The one credible AI-enabled disruption path: an adjacent vendor uses models to build a rival library." | **"One attacker is real: an adjacent vendor cloning the library with models. It still takes five steps and a decade — and the contracts can stop it."** |

**What the third column does that the second doesn't:** leads with the claim, cuts the connective tissue, lands a short punch, and earns its contrast. Notice none of them add words — the MBB version is usually *shorter* than the de-slopped one.

## Voice calibration

The target voice, described by its properties rather than by any one deck: **declarative, compressed, contrast-driven, quantified, and unafraid of a fragment.** Lines that hit the register (illustrative):

- A category claim in three words, full stop.
- "Does your best work. Faster."
- "The real asset is judgement, not the artifact."
- "Built for the workflow, not retro-fitted from a chat tool."
- "Repeatable, but rigid." / "What you delivered, not how."
- "Opinionated where it needs to be; flexible where you are."
- "Cut the per-stage assessment from days to under thirty minutes."

*Tone in one line: state the claim, cut the padding, earn the contrast, and let one short punch carry the slide.*
